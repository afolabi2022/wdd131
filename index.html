Liberia

Liberia is a country on the West African coast. It was founded in 1847. The country has beautiful beaches, rich natural resources, and a diverse culture. It is bordered by Sierra Leone to the west, Guinea to the north, Côte d'Ivoire to the east, and the Atlantic Ocean to the south. Liberia has a population of around 5 million people and its capital and largest city is Monrovia. The country was founded by freed American and Caribbean slaves in the early 19th century, and it is Africa's first and oldest modern republic. Liberia has a rich cultural heritage
